The Basic Requirements For Entry (Nursery)

  • The Child Must Be At Least 3 Years Old
  • Birth Certificate Of The Child
  • 2(Two) passport photos Of The Child
  • A Sign Affidavit Of The Child's Sponsor
  • A Complete Fill Application Form Either Online Or On Paper
The cost of the application fee for the nursery school is D200. It can be paid cash at the school only. We advise all parents to apply on time to avoid delay.

Lower Basic Entry Requirements

  • Rusults Showing Grades and Subjects From Previous School.
  • Birth Certificate Of The Pupil.
  • 2(Two) passport photos Of The pupil
  • A Sign Affidavit Of The Child's pupil
  • Certificate From Previous School(if any)
  • A D250 Application Form.

Cultural Party
Every year Mobe-ta nursery and lower basic School organize a cultural party for all the children to take part. It is organize after the graduation ceremony. Pupils will put on their traditional dresses and come to the party. Most pupils will take part in entertainment such as acting drama,singing traditional songs, traditional stories, traditional dancing compitition, musical chair and so on .
Graduation Day Ceremony
The graduation ceremony day which is organize every end of the last term in june. All pupils take part in it but all nursery 4 pupils graduate. The graduation ceremony is alaways fun and interesting pupils are enguage in acting drama, singing, dancing, residing poems, and cultural activities.
