The following rules and regulations are for the attention of all parents/guardians and pupils. Parents or guardians should ensure that their children understand them. The disciplinary committee will monitor adherence to this rules and regulations. The rules and regulations are in cognizance of mobeta child protection policy convention on the rights of the child. (CRC) ans allother child right related issues. thus its applicationwill be in line with the policy and in promotion of alternative disciplinary measures.

1. The school days starts at 8:15am from monday to friday. Pupils should be in school on time.

1.2 Pupils coming after 8:35am respectively will be termed as late comers.

1.3 Pupils coming to school late will have their names written down and made to pick litters after school and be detained for an extra one hour.

1.4 The school gate will be closed at 9am respectively. students coming after these time will no be allow to enter the school.

1. Pupils are expected to be of good behaviour at all times. They should be obedient, polite and respectful towards teachers, prefects, visitors and one another.All parents should help their children acquire good manners and positive attitudes.

2. English will be the language of communication at all times with everyone in the school.Any student caught speaking vernacular will be detained after school to do some cleaning exercise in the school.

3. Pupils must wear the school's uniform during school hour except for physical education classes when the house colours should be worn. Boys and girls should tuck their shiets/ blouse as long as they are with their school uniforms. Pullovers, preferable in the school colours may be worn during the cold season. Jean shits and jackets will not be allow. Black shoes and white socks will only be allow.slippers and other coloured shoes will also not be allow.

General neatness is emphasized.

a. No student should wear Tight-fitting or gun mouth trousers to school.

b.Graduating students should respect the school's dress code on graduation day.

  • school uniform worn on that day school be the correct one.
  • High heeled shoes will be not be allow.

c. All new intakes must buy uniformsand books from the school.

4. Only simple hairstyle are allowed e.g braids, low haircuts etc. no hair extension, jerry curi or weird haircuts. Girls should put on undesirable fashions in the school. They must dress decently while in the school premises. No extremes of fashions.

5. Pupils/students are not allow to carry mobile phones to school. if seen with anyone, it willbe seized and can only be returned at the end of thye academic year.

Pupils are expected to attend school and closses regularly and punctually. Truancy and loitering in the school compound will be check and culprits or to be expelled when persistent.

Payment of schhol fees must be made on or before the first day of each term.

  • a. Any parent wishing to withdraw his or her child must do so within the first week of the term beyond which fees must be paid.
  • If a child is withdrawn either by the school or the parent after patmentof fees, the fees paid will not be refunded.

8. All pupils must take part in extracurricular activities if their health parmits.

9. No visitors for pupils are allow during school hours expect in a special circumstances and by permission from the office. Pupils will not be allowed to go out of the school till the end of the school day expect if they are allowed for a good reasons. They must also stay whitn the premises in emergency situations for their safety.

11. Pupils should not eat in the classrooms.

Pupils should not be in possession of weapons and dangerous objects e.g knives, razors, razors, guns,etc.

The following offences listed below are deemed to be serious, intolerable and will be dealt with as determined by the disciplinary committee.

  • a. Striking or assaulting a teacher or pupils.
  • b. Using foul language or insulting a teacher or pupil.
  • c. Fighting or bullying.
  • d. Willful damage to school property and the environment.
  • e. Stealing.
  • f. Smooking cigaretts and drug taking.

Gross misbehaviour may result in expulsion
