The History Of Mobe-ta School.

MOBE-TA NURSERY SCHOOL was founded by Edna Turner in 1980. It started as a small school with only 58 pupils by then. Mobeta school was the only school in Dippakunda in the 1980s but in thoes some parents were not familiar in education. The school was purposely build for the poor to start school with little or no payment. As she was growing old, she handed the school to her son called Musa Javis Turner who took it as the proprietor. Edna died in 1997(May Her Sole Rest In Perfect pease), that was when her son Musa Javis Turner took it as complete ownership. Musa took the school and transformed it to a big, wonderful and lovely school(from nursery one to four.). musa always wanted all the children in the communication to take part in education as he always said to the parent eduaction is the key to become succesful in life.

Many parents agreed with him but some did not. Parents where hard to convice in the community because so of them did not have the opportuity to go to school. As time goes on many agree with him. The school began to have more and more kid comming . the school was getting bigger and bigger as time goes on and so many parents began to bring to kid to the school to aquire eduaction. As from today so many students are successfully in their feild of studies. unfortutely the sad news happened in september 2013 that was when Musa Turner died ( May His Sole Rest In Perfect Peace).

School location

Mobeta nursery and lower basic school is located at Dippakunda,Fillan street in serekunda. mobe-ta school is the only nursery and primary school in dippakunda. Dippakunda is a small town in serekunda. The school was build in dippakunda to help the commuincation people living around. The school was mainly build to help the poor people to aquired education. Decades ago their was no school in dippakunda and so many parents cannot affort to pay for their kid to aquired education. Mobe-ta school has help so many children to gain eduaction and lots of our student are now in their professional skills of studies. Many have been become successful in life.

About The School Environment.

The school is 50meters long and 40 meters wide. Their are 4 nurseries classes and 6 primaries classes in the primary. 6(six) toilets in all 3 in the primary, 1 for boys 1 for girls and the one for the staffs as well. The same goes for the nursery.30 chairs and tables in each class in the nursery and 15 to 20 chairs and table in each class in the primary. Their are two offices one for the nursery and one for the primary. A playing ground for both the nursery and primary. A computer lap for computer training and computer expert teacher to train the pupils.
